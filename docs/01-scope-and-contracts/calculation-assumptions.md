# Calculation Assumptions

## Money Health Score Categories

- Emergency Fund: target is 6 months of total monthly expenses.
- Insurance: term target is 10x annual income, health target is INR 500000.
- Investments: compares current investments to 50 percent of annual income benchmark.
- Debt: debt score penalizes high debt-to-income ratio.
- Tax Planning: checks utilization of 80C, 80D, and NPS buckets.
- Retirement: checks annual savings trajectory vs corpus need proxy.

## Aggregate Score

- Total score is mean of all category scores, clamped to 0-100.

## Grade Bands

- 80-100: EXCELLENT
- 65-79: GOOD
- 50-64: FAIR
- 0-49: NEEDS ATTENTION

## Constraints

- This is educational guidance, not regulated investment advice.
- Formulas are simplified for hackathon MVP speed and explainability.
